Featured Museums

Check out some of the most popular museums across India and start your journey into the past.

National Museum, Delhi

Discover the vast collection of Indian art, culture, and heritage preserved at the National Museum in Delhi.

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya, Mumbai

Explore Mumbai's premier museum showcasing ancient artifacts, art, and relics from India's rich history.

Indian Museum, Kolkata

Visit the oldest museum in India and one of the most comprehensive museums in Asia, located in Kolkata.

Rail Museum, New Delhi

Step into the history of Indian Railways and explore the collection of vintage trains and rail artifacts.

Albert Hall Museum, Jaipur

The oldest museum in Rajasthan, known for its Indo-Saracenic architecture and extensive collection of artifacts.

Salar Jung Museum, Hyderabad

Famous for its rare artifacts and art collections from various civilizations around the world.

Egyptian Gallery, Indian Museum

Explore ancient Egyptian artifacts, including mummies, in the Indian Museum's Egyptian Gallery in Kolkata.

Dr. Bhau Daji Lad Museum, Mumbai

Mumbai's oldest museum with collections highlighting the city’s culture and industrial past.
